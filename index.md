# Privacy Policy — தமிழுடன் விளையாடு

**Last updated:** June 7, 2026

## Overview
தமிழுடன் விளையாடு is a fully offline educational quiz app. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- No account registration required
- No internet connection required
- No data is sent to any server
- No analytics or tracking

## Data Stored Locally
The app stores the following data **only on your device**:
- Game progress (level completion, scores, stars)
- Game settings (sound/voice preferences)

This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy
This app is safe for children. We do not collect any information from children or adults.
